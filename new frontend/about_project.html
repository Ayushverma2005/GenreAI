Deep Learning Core

Our system leverages a modified ResNet-34 architecture, specifically tuned for spectral image data. By treating audio Mel-spectrograms as visual patterns, the model identifies complex textures in frequency distribution that define musical genres.

Signal Processing

Before reaching the neural network, audio is processed through a multi-stage pipeline: Fast Fourier Transform (FFT), Mel-scaling, and Logarithmic compression. This mimics human auditory perception for better accuracy.
